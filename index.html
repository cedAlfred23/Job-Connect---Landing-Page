About Us

We started this project during our time at Holberton School as a portfolio project. Our goal was to create a platform that helps job seekers find the perfect job opportunities. Inspired by our own experiences navigating through job search platforms, we aimed to develop a user-friendly and efficient solution. This project is a culmination of our hard work and dedication to learning and applying new technologies.
